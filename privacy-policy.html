Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how it is used.

Effective Date: May 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: May 1, 2025

01

Who We Are

Kings of Queens Detailing is a mobile automotive detailing and ceramic coating business operating in Queens, NY and surrounding areas including Long Island and other New York boroughs. We are owned and operated by Aidan Friel.

This Privacy Policy applies to all individuals who contact us, submit their information through our marketing channels, or communicate with us via SMS text message.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you interact with our business:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • General location or service area
  • Vehicle type or detailing service interest
  • Any information you voluntarily share through SMS or web forms

This information is collected when you respond to our Google Ads, visit our website, interact with a physical flyer, or contact us directly.

03

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your service inquiries and provide quotes
  • To schedule and confirm detailing appointments
  • To send SMS communications that you have explicitly opted into
  • To follow up on services rendered
  • To improve our customer experience

📵 We do not use your personal information for any third-party marketing, data selling, or advertising profiling. Your data is used only to service your request with Kings of Queens Detailing.

04

SMS Messaging & Text Communications

Kings of Queens Detailing may send you SMS text messages for the purpose of providing quotes, scheduling services, and following up on inquiries. SMS messages are only sent to individuals who have explicitly opted in.

How you opt in: After engaging with one of our marketing channels (Google Ads, our website, or a flyer), you will be presented with a clear opt-in prompt. You must reply YES to confirm your consent before any messages are sent.

How to opt out: You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. Upon receiving your STOP request, all messages will immediately cease.

Need help? Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at the information below.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency will vary based on your inquiry status. We do not share your phone number or any SMS opt-in data with third parties.

05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Kings of Queens Detailing does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

Your information will never be:

  • Sold to data brokers or advertisers
  • Shared with partner companies for marketing
  • Used to build advertising profiles
  • Disclosed to any outside party without your consent

We may use trusted service platforms (such as SMS delivery providers) solely to facilitate communication with you. These platforms are bound by their own privacy and data protection policies and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — primarily to complete your service request and maintain basic business records. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

07

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Withdraw consent for any data usage at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly using the information below.
