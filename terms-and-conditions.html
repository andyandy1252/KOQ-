How to Opt Out

You can cancel your SMS subscription at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us.

Reply STOP to Unsubscribe

After you send the SMS message STOP to our number, we will send you one final confirmation message. After that, you will receive no further SMS messages from Kings of Queens Detailing unless you choose to opt back in.

Other accepted opt-out keywords: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT.

You may also contact us directly by phone to request removal from our SMS list.
